# Winter Indoor Championship

Join us for our annual Winter Indoor Championship! This tournament is open to all skill levels with divisions for youth, adult, and senior archers.

## Event Details
- **Date**: January 18, 2025
- **Time**: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **Location**: Indoor Range
- **Registration Deadline**: January 15, 2025
- **Entry Fee**: $25 members, $35 non-members

## Divisions
- Youth (Under 18): Recurve, Compound
- Adult (18-49): Recurve, Compound, Traditional
- Senior (50+): Recurve, Compound, Traditional

## Format
- 60 arrows at 18 meters
- NFAA indoor target face
- Awards for top 3 in each division
- Lunch provided for all participants
