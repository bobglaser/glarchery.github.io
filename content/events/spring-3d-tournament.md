# Spring 3D Tournament

Kick off the outdoor season with our exciting 3D tournament! Navigate our challenging woodland course featuring lifelike animal targets.

## Event Details
- **Date**: March 15, 2025
- **Time**: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- **Location**: Outdoor 3D Course
- **Registration Deadline**: March 10, 2025
- **Entry Fee**: $30 members, $40 non-members

## Course Information
- 30 targets through wooded terrain
- Distances from 10-50 yards
- Unmarked yardages
- Various shooting positions and angles

## Divisions
- **Traditional**: Longbow, recurve (no sights)
- **Instinctive**: Any bow, no sights or release aids
- **Bowhunter**: Compound with hunting setup
- **Open**: Any equipment allowed

## Schedule
- 8:00 AM: Registration and equipment check
- 9:00 AM: Safety briefing
- 9:30 AM: Tournament begins
- 2:00 PM: Scoring and awards
- 3:00 PM: Event concludes

## What to Bring
- Your own equipment
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Water and snacks
- Insect repellent (recommended)
