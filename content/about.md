# About Precision Archery Club

Founded in 1985, Precision Archery Club has been serving the local archery community for nearly four decades. What started as a small group of enthusiasts has grown into one of the region's premier archery facilities.

## Our Facilities

### Indoor Range
- 20-yard climate-controlled range
- 12 shooting lanes
- Professional lighting system
- Available year-round

### Outdoor Range
- 50-yard field archery course
- 3D target course through wooded terrain
- Traditional target butts at various distances
- Seasonal availability (March - November)

## Programs & Services

- **Beginner Classes**: Learn the fundamentals in a safe, supportive environment
- **Advanced Training**: Technique refinement and competitive preparation
- **Youth Programs**: After-school and summer programs for ages 8-17
- **Adult Leagues**: Recreational and competitive leagues for all skill levels
- **Equipment Services**: Sales, repairs, and custom tuning

## Safety & Certification

All our instructors are certified by USA Archery and maintain current safety certifications. We follow strict range safety protocols to ensure a secure environment for all participants.
