## Jenny Kim
**Youth Program Coordinator**

Jenny is the heart of Grand Ledge Archery's youth development pipeline. Her patient, encouraging teaching style has introduced hundreds of young people in the Grand Ledge community to the sport — many of whom have gone on to compete at the state and national level.

### Achievements

- USA Archery Level 3 Certified Instructor
- Youth Program Development certification
- Former collegiate archer — Michigan State University
- 10+ years of youth coaching experience
- Developed Grand Ledge's award-winning beginner curriculum

### Specialties

- Beginning archery instruction (ages 8 and up)
- Youth range safety and protocols
- Parent-child archery programs
- School partnership and camp programs

### Coaching Philosophy

> "Archery is one of the few sports where a kid can walk in on day one, follow the steps, and hit the target. That first moment of success — that's the hook. My job is to build on it, keep it fun, and watch them grow into athletes who believe in themselves."
