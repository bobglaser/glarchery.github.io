## Mike Thompson
**Compound Bow Specialist**

Mike brings 20 years of competitive compound archery experience to Grand Ledge. As a former professional archer who competed at the world level, he understands what it takes to perform under pressure — and how to teach those skills to the next generation.

### Achievements

- 2015 World Field Archery Champion
- 5x National Field Champion
- Professional Archery Association (PAA) member
- USA Archery Level 3 Certified Instructor
- Equipment tuning specialist — certified by multiple bow manufacturers

### Specialties

- Compound bow setup, tuning, and customization
- Field archery and 3D target shooting
- Equipment selection for competitive archers
- Mental game and pre-competition routines

### Coaching Philosophy

> "Precision is the product of understanding — understanding your equipment, your body, and your mind. I teach archers to know their gear as well as they know their own shot. When those two things align, consistency follows."
