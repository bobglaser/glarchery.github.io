## Sarah Martinez
**Head Coach & Program Director**

Sarah has been the cornerstone of Grand Ledge Archery for over 15 years. She joined the program as a junior archer and worked her way up through every level of the sport before taking the helm as Head Coach. Under her leadership, Grand Ledge Archery has become one of Michigan's most respected scholastic programs.

### Achievements

- 2018 National Indoor Champion — Recurve
- 3x Michigan State Champion (2016, 2017, 2018)
- USA Archery Level 4 Certified Instructor
- Olympic Training Center coaching certification
- Coached 12 individual state champions at Grand Ledge

### Specialties

- Recurve technique and form refinement
- Mental preparation and competition strategy
- Youth athlete development
- Advanced shooting mechanics

### Coaching Philosophy

> "Every archer has a unique natural style. My job is to help them discover it, build the technical foundation around it, and then get out of the way and let them shoot. Confidence comes from repetition and trust in your own form."
