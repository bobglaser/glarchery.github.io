Meet Our Coaches

🏹

Sarah Mitchell

Head Coach

Coach Mitchell has led the Grand Ledge Archery Team for over eight years, bringing a background in competitive recurve archery and youth development. She is a USA Archery Level 3 certified instructor and has guided multiple archers to state-level competition. Her coaching philosophy centers on patience, consistency, and building mental focus alongside physical skill.
