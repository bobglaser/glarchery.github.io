Meet Our Coaches

🏹

Sarah Mitchell

Head Coach

Coach Mitchell has led the Grand Ledge Archery Team for over eight years, bringing a background in competitive recurve archery and youth development. She is a USA Archery Level 3 certified instructor and has guided multiple archers to state-level competition. Her coaching philosophy centers on patience, consistency, and building mental focus alongside physical skill.

🏹

Maria Chen

Assistant Coach & Safety Officer

Coach Chen brings fifteen years of range safety experience and a passion for inclusive athletics. She holds certifications in both USA Archery instruction and first aid. Maria coordinates all safety protocols for practices and tournaments and runs the team's beginner orientation program each fall.

🏹

James Kowalski

Assistant Coach

Coach Kowalski joined the team in 2020 after a decade of competitive compound bow shooting. A Grand Ledge High School alumnus, he returned to give back to the program that shaped his love of the sport. He specializes in equipment tuning, form correction, and helping newer archers build confidence on the line.
