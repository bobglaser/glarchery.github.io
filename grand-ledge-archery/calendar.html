Event Calendar

2026–2027 Season

May 10, 2026

Spring Invitational Tournament

Annual spring tournament hosted at Grand Ledge High School. Open to all NASP member schools. Registration closes May 3rd.

June 14–18, 2026

Summer Archery Camp

Week-long day camp for students entering grades 5–12. All equipment provided. Focus on fundamentals, safety, and fun.

September 6, 2026

Fall Season Practices Begin

Weekly practices resume every Tuesday and Thursday, 3:30–5:00 PM in the high school gymnasium. New archers welcome — no experience needed.

November 14, 2026

State Qualifier

Regional qualifying round for the Michigan State Archery Championship. Top scorers advance to the state finals in January.

January 23, 2027

Michigan State Championship

State-level NASP tournament in Lansing. Qualified archers compete for individual and team titles.
